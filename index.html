What is SensorX?

A hands-on technical club focused on Arduino, sensors, and embedded systems — open to all students from zero experience to expert level.

SensorX was founded by students who wanted a practical learning community around electronics and hardware programming. We believe the best way to learn is to build real projects that solve real problems.

Whether you've never touched a circuit or you're already building autonomous robots — SensorX has a structured path for you. Our curriculum covers everything from basic electronics fundamentals to advanced IoT systems and sensor fusion.

Members get access to learning resources, workshop opportunities, real project experience, peer mentorship, and verified completion certificates upon finishing each course module.
